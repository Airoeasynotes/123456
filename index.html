Who We Are

Building the future together

Founded on May 29, 2020 by Surya (Hemateja) and Sriya, Airme Organization began as a passion project between two developers who believed technology should be accessible to all.

Today, we've grown into a thriving community collaborating on innovative solutions that lower barriers to technology creation.

Inclusive Community

All skill levels welcome. We learn and grow together, fostering a supportive environment.

Practical Innovation

Real solutions for real problems with minimal overhead, focusing on impact.

Open Collaboration

Transparent development process with shared ownership and open communication.

Sustainable Growth

Projects designed for long-term maintainability and community well-being.
